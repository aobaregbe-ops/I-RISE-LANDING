The Problem

A Global Crisis

Over a billion people are chronically dysregulated. The economic and human cost is staggering — and no existing solution addresses the root cause.

1B+
People worldwide with chronic stress, anxiety or burnout
$1T
Lost productivity per year globally (World Economic Forum, 2023)
€600B
Annual cost to Europe — approx. 4% of GDP (OECD, 2022)

The autonomic nervous system regulates arousal, attention, emotion and physiology simultaneously. When chronically dysregulated, all downstream systems are affected at once.

🧠
Psychological
Emotional instability emerges when the nervous system remains in chronic threat activation.
Depression & anxiety · Burnout & PTSD
⚙️
Cognitive
Executive function degrades when autonomic load stays high and prefrontal regulation drops.
Concentration loss · Decision errors
🫀
Physical
Sleep, immunity and cardiovascular systems follow autonomic regulation — not conscious control.
Sleep disorders · Cardiovascular risk
📈
Economic
Performance under chronic dysregulation is compensatory, not sustainable.
Absenteeism · Productivity loss
Mental health symptoms are downstream effects.
Autonomic dysregulation is the upstream biological driver — and no existing solution targets it directly.
